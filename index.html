My First Day at The Iron Yard

Today was a pretty good day, a little differnt but definitely informative. We were exposed to many concepts from different angles. Today everything sailed smoothly and softly, but it's only the first day. What about next week? So far I'm excited and I'm sure I'll stay that way for the duration of the class or until I get to the point of no understanding.

I saw some of my old classmates today. I was kind of hurt but I know I'm not that far behind. Today was a good day. The class is very focused and driven, which I think helps us envision the end goal of becoming Software Engineers. Ryan, our instructor, is very helpful and doesn't mind answering questions. The work he gives is serious but I think it will help us become great developers. First day down and 59 more to go!

Tamarcus Summers
